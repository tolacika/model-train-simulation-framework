## Track Geometry and Spacing

* **R1 to R2** center spacing: 180 mm
* **R2 to R3** center spacing: 415 mm
* **R3 to R5** center spacing: 1125 mm

### G-Scale Standard:

* **Track gauge**: 45 mm
* **Tie width**: \~48.5 mm
* **Roadbed width**: \~89 mm
